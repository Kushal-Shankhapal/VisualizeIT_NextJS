Operator Precedence Guide

Operators have different priorities. Higher precedence operators are processed first.

^   Exponentiation Highest (3)
* /   Multiply, Divide Medium (2)
+ -   Add, Subtract Lowest (1)
How it works: When an operator is encountered, all stack operators with equal or higher precedence are popped to output before the new one is pushed.
